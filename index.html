OSINT Services:

What is OSINT? Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) involves collecting and analyzing publicly available information to identify potential security risks. This includes data from social media, forums, websites, and other publicly accessible sources.

Importance of OSINT: OSINT helps organizations understand their exposure to external threats, identify leaked information, and protect against social engineering attacks. By leveraging OSINT, businesses can proactively mitigate risks and enhance their security posture.

I provide comprehensive OSINT services to help organizations uncover leaked information, breached emails, and other vulnerabilities. My services include thorough investigations to identify and secure exposed data, protecting organizations from external attacks and social engineering threats.
